Content

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

KILMV Gaming will stream about Mobile Legends, a sample gameplay and guides. The streamers will be Vincent Aldin Hamto, David Lance Perez, Karl Vincent Salvo, Miles Sangilan, and Iris Arianna Callanta. Our content will revolve around the famous mobile game, MOBILE LEGENDS: BANG BANG. We will share some tips and facts about it and how to use specific heroes, from beginner tips, to pro tips.

Goal

Gaming has been the escape route of many students. We aim to guide those beginners who have low self-esteem when it comes to playing games. To do this, we will conduct a stream wherein we will demonstrate the gameplay of Mobile Legends: Bang Bang.
